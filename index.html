About

Thacapple started as a small space focused on actuarial and risk-based work.

Over time, it evolved into a broader platform where insurance, actuarial, finance, and applied probability could be explored through real analysis and direct experience.

What remained constant was the intention: to keep thinking practical, clear, and shareable.

Today, Thacapple is not a finished product. It is a platform that grows with the people who build and question within it.
